Rainer

Rainer helps you deal with namespaces of versioned key/value pairs called "commits". It includes a variety of APIs that can collectively provide a full audit trail, concurrency-safe updates, local caching, immediate cluster notifications, and easy client integration. The Rainer APIs can be used independently, or they can be used together as part of a powerful configuration management system.
